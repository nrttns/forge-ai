# Contributing to Forge AI

Forge AI is currently in a governed public-preview stage. External code and documentation contributions are not accepted yet.

This temporary boundary protects:

- Human Governance authority;
- the separation between Forge AI Target Project truth and AI-DOS product truth;
- active normalization and self-hosting work;
- provenance of architectural decisions and validation evidence;
- future licensing decisions.

You may open a non-security issue to report a reproducible defect or documentation problem. Opening an issue does not transfer ownership, grant a license, or authorize implementation. Unsolicited pull requests may be closed without review.

Do not use public issues for vulnerabilities or sensitive information. Follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

The contribution policy may change only after Human Governance adopts explicit contribution and licensing terms.
