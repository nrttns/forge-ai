# Contributing to Forge AI

Forge AI is open-source software licensed under the Apache License 2.0. External code and documentation contributions are not currently accepted during the governed public-preview stage.

This temporary contribution boundary protects:

- Human Governance authority;
- the separation between Forge AI Target Project truth and AI-DOS product truth;
- active normalization and self-hosting work;
- provenance of architectural decisions and validation evidence;
- controlled contribution review and licensing compliance.

The Apache-2.0 license permits use, modification, and distribution under its terms. It does not require the maintainers to accept, review, or merge a proposed contribution.

You may open a non-security issue to report a reproducible defect or documentation problem. Opening an issue does not transfer ownership or authorize implementation. Unsolicited pull requests may be closed without review.

Do not use public issues for vulnerabilities or sensitive information. Follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

The contribution policy may change only after Human Governance adopts explicit contribution terms.
