# AI-DOS Review Template Family

This document is the semantic entry and boundary contract for `docs/AI-DOS/Templates/Review/`.

## Purpose

The family provides reusable structures for evaluating a bounded subject against declared criteria, recording observations, findings, objections, rationale, unresolved matters, result, and a non-authorizing recommendation.

## Component

- `ReviewTemplate.md`

## Authority Boundary

Validation artifacts may be consumed as evidence but do not become automatic review conclusions. A favorable review does not certify, approve, promote, canonicalize, release, authorize execution, or mutate Target state.

Review criteria, reviewer authority, result vocabulary, and evidence provenance must be explicitly supplied or governed.

## Safe Stop

Stop when subject, scope, review authority, reviewer identity, criteria, evidence, provenance, result vocabulary, protected boundary, or intended consumer is missing or ambiguous.
