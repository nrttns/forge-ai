# AI-DOS Validation Template Family

This document is the semantic entry and boundary contract for `docs/AI/Templates/Validation/`.

## Purpose

The family provides reusable structures for bounded evidence, findings, risks, blockers, result reporting, and recommended next action.

## Component

- `ValidationTemplate.md`

## Authority Boundary

Validation reports record evidence-backed findings only. The family does not own truth, validation rules, quality gates, review, certification, approval, promotion, canonicalization, release, execution authorization, or Target-state mutation.

A passing validation result does not imply review acceptance, certification, approval, completion, promotion, or release.

## Safe Stop

Stop when subject, scope, owner, rule source, evidence source, acceptance criteria, provenance, protected boundary, or intended consumer is missing or ambiguous.
