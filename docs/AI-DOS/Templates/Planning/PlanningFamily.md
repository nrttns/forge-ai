# AI-DOS Planning Template Family

This document is the semantic entry and boundary contract for `docs/AI-DOS/Templates/Planning/`.

## Purpose

The family provides reusable structures for vision, roadmap, phase, stage, and historical-capability artifacts when an owning Target or product authority explicitly requests them.

## Components

- `VisionTemplate.md`
- `RoadmapTemplate.md`
- `PhaseTemplate.md`
- `StageTemplate.md`
- `HistoricalCapabilityTemplate.md`

These templates are peers. They do not establish a mandatory planning hierarchy.

## Authority Boundary

The family owns reusable document structure only. It does not own Target vision, roadmap, planning, state, lifecycle, source of truth, task selection, workflow routing, execution authorization, approval, certification, promotion, release, or Target-state mutation.

No Forge AI path, ProjectStatus, DevelopmentPhases, Roadmap, phase, stage, capability, sprint, or self-hosting convention is required by default.

## Safe Stop

Stop when purpose, owner, authority, provenance, lifecycle, Target planning source, protected boundary, or validation requirement is missing or ambiguous.
