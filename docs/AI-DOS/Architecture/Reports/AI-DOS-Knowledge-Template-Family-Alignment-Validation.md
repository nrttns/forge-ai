- source statements;
- observed facts;
- asserted claims;
- validated findings;
- decisions;
- assumptions;
- hypotheses;
- inferences;
- contradictions;
- deprecated or superseded claims.

Result: PASS.

## Provenance and Evidence Validation

The component requires:

- source identity and type;
- source location and owner;
- retrieval or observation date;
- freshness;
- integrity notes;
- source authority status;
- evidence basis;
- counter-evidence;
- missing evidence;
- confidence and rationale.

Source presence, evidence presence, and confidence do not automatically establish truth or authority.

Result: PASS.

## Canonical and Lifecycle Status Validation

Canonical, approved, promoted, persistent, retired, expired, and superseded states may be recorded only when supplied by the applicable authority. The template cannot create those states.

Result: PASS.
